# Privacy Policy

**Haven Player** does not collect, upload, or share any personal data.

- **Local data only**: Playback position, preferences (e.g. resume, always on top, window transparency), and playlist are stored only on your device in the app’s local data. Nothing is sent to our servers or third parties.
- **No account**: The app does not require an account. There is no sign-in or user profile.
- **No tracking**: We do not use cookies, analytics, or similar technologies to track you.
- **User-selected files**: The app reads only the video, audio, or subtitle files you explicitly open or drag in. It does not access other files or folders on your device.

If you have questions about privacy, contact: **kldtks@live.com**.

# 隐私政策

**Haven Player** 不收集、不上传、不分享任何个人数据。

- **仅本地数据**：续播位置、偏好设置（如续播、窗口置顶、半透明）及播放列表仅保存在您本机的应用数据中，不会发送到我们的服务器或第三方。
- **无账号**：应用不需要注册或登录，无用户档案。
- **无追踪**：我们不使用 Cookie、统计或类似技术追踪您。
- **用户选择的文件**：应用仅读取您主动打开或拖入的视频、音频、字幕文件，不会访问您设备上的其他文件或文件夹。

如有隐私相关疑问，请联系：**kldtks@live.com**。
